Salt Sana Empowerment For Nkana

Empowering Students, Youths and Marketeers through community development, skills training and economic support.

About The Platform

This platform allows residents of Nkana Constituency to register for empowerment programs targeting students, youths and marketeers in order to improve the economy of Nkana.

Who Can Register

Students

Students can register for educational support and scholarship programs.

Youths

Youth empowerment through skills training, entrepreneurship and development.

Marketeers

Market traders can access business support and economic empowerment.
